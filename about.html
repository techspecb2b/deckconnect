Our Story

From Passion to Profession

Deck Connect LLC was founded with a simple vision: to create beautiful outdoor living spaces that bring families together and stand the test of time. What started as a passion for craftsmanship has grown into a trusted name in premium deck building across Northern Illinois and Southern Wisconsin.

We believe that your outdoor space should be an extension of your home—a place where memories are made, celebrations are held, and everyday moments become special. That's why we approach every project with the same dedication and attention to detail, whether it's a simple deck refresh or a complete outdoor transformation.

Our team brings together years of experience, continuous training on the latest techniques, and a genuine love for what we do. We're not just building decks; we're building connections—between families and their outdoor spaces, between quality materials and expert craftsmanship, and between our company and the communities we serve.

Our Mission

Creating Spaces Where Life Happens

At Deck Connect, our mission is simple: to design and build outdoor living spaces that enhance your lifestyle, increase your home's value, and give you a beautiful place to create lasting memories with family and friends.

We're committed to using sustainable materials, supporting our local communities, and setting the standard for excellence in the deck building industry. Every project is an opportunity to exceed expectations and earn a customer for life.
